Is eRecyclo free to use?

add

Yes. Creating an account and listing your devices is completely free. eRecyclo earns a platform fee from vendors and collectors — users always pay nothing and receive the full agreed value.

Who can use eRecyclo?

add

Anyone in India with old or broken electronic devices. Individuals, households, offices, and businesses of any size are welcome. You simply need a valid email address and a photo of your device to get started.

What types of devices can I recycle?

add

We accept 13 categories of e-waste: smartphones, laptops, desktop computers, tablets, monitors and TVs, batteries and chargers, computer peripherals, audio and cameras, storage and networking equipment, gaming consoles, large appliances, small appliances (including microwaves), and cables and components.

Do I need to clean or wipe my device before pickup?

add

We strongly recommend performing a factory reset and removing personal data before handing your device over. While our vendors are certified professionals bound by data handling policies, clearing your device is best practice for your own peace of mind.

Pricing & Payment

How does the AI estimate my device's value?

add

Our AI identifies the category and type of device from the photo, then cross-references current market rates for recoverable raw materials (metals, components). The estimate is a range — the exact final price is determined when a vendor makes their offer, based on closer inspection of condition and details.

Can a vendor change the price after I accept?

add

No. Once you accept a vendor's offer, the price is locked. The exact amount is credited to your wallet the moment your device is verified at pickup. There is no renegotiation, no underpaying on delivery.

How do I get the money from my wallet?

add

Your wallet balance can be transferred to your registered bank account directly from the Wallet section of your dashboard. Withdrawals are processed within 1–2 business days. There are no transfer fees.

What if I'm not happy with any vendor offer?

add

You are never obligated to accept an offer. You can reject it and wait for other vendors to bid, or you can close the listing and try again later. There is no penalty for rejecting offers.

Pickup & Collection

Is the pickup free?

add

Yes. Doorstep collection is completely free for users. Our collectors are compensated directly by the platform per delivery. You will never pay a logistics or service fee.

How does the OTP verification work?

add

When a collector arrives, a unique One-Time Password is generated in your dashboard. You share this OTP with the collector verbally — they enter it into their app to confirm handoff. This ensures your device is only transferred to the correct, verified collector assigned to your job.

What if the collector doesn't arrive?

add

If a collector fails to show up, you can report the issue through the platform. Your accepted offer remains valid. The job will be reassigned to another collector, and your payment is unaffected.

Account & Privacy

Is my personal data safe?

add

Yes. We collect only the minimum information needed to operate the platform. Your data is never sold or shared with third parties for marketing purposes. Read our Privacy Policy for full details.

Can I delete my account?

add

Yes. You can request account deletion by contacting us at hello@erecyclo.in. We will deactivate your account and delete your personal data within 14 days, subject to any active transactions being completed.
