eRecyclo is India's AI-powered platform that connects individuals and businesses with certified recyclers — making responsible e-waste disposal rewarding, simple, and transparent.

warning The Problem We Solve

India generates 3.2 million tonnes of e-waste every year.

Electronic waste is the world's fastest-growing waste stream. Devices discarded improperly leach toxic heavy metals — lead, mercury, cadmium — into soil and water, causing irreversible environmental damage.

Despite this, over 95% of India's e-waste ends up in the informal sector — processed unsafely, with no oversight, no fair pricing, and no environmental accountability. eRecyclo exists to change that.
