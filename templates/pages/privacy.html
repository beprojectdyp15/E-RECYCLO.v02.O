Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how eRecyclo ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our platform. By using eRecyclo, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information when you use eRecyclo:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, and password (hashed) when you register.
  • Profile Information: Phone number, address, and profile photo if provided.
  • Device Listings: Photos and descriptions of devices you submit for recycling.
  • Location Data: Your pickup address and, for collectors, approximate GPS location when actively on a job (with your permission).
  • Transaction Data: Offer amounts, accepted values, wallet balance, and payout history.
  • Device & Usage Data: Browser type, IP address, pages visited, and timestamps — used for security and platform improvement.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Create and manage your account and listings
  • Match your listings with verified recycling vendors
  • Dispatch verified collectors for doorstep pickups
  • Process wallet credits and payout transactions
  • Send transactional notifications (offer received, pickup confirmed, payment credited)
  • Detect fraud and maintain platform security
  • Improve AI identification accuracy (using anonymised device images)
  • Comply with applicable Indian laws and regulations

3. Information We Do Not Collect or Share

We are committed to minimal data collection. Specifically:

  • We do not sell your personal data to any third party, ever.
  • We do not share your information with advertisers.
  • We do not store the data from your devices beyond what is needed for the transaction.
  • We do not access the contents of any device you submit for recycling.

4. Information Shared with Platform Participants

To operate the platform, limited information is shared:

  • With Vendors: Your device listing details (photos, category, description). Your full name and contact details are only shared after you accept an offer.
  • With Collectors: Your pickup address and OTP after a job is assigned. Your full name is shared for identification on arrival.
  • All vendors and collectors are verified participants bound by confidentiality obligations.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS), hashed passwords (never stored in plain text), and access controls limiting who can view user data internally. While no system is 100% secure, we continuously review and update our security practices.

6. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. Completed transaction records are retained for 3 years to comply with financial regulations. Device listing photos are deleted within 90 days of a transaction being completed. You may request deletion of your account at any time.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate information in your profile
  • Request deletion of your account and personal data
  • Withdraw consent for non-essential data uses at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at hello@erecyclo.in.

8. Cookies

We use session cookies to keep you logged in and functional cookies to remember your preferences. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but this may affect platform functionality.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the date at the top of this page and, for material changes, notify you via email or an in-platform notification.

10. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at hello@erecyclo.in.
