Your Journey as a User

Turn old electronics into cash in 3 steps

1

Register & Upload Your Device

Create a free account and navigate to the Upload section. Take or upload a clear photo of your device — our AI scans it automatically. It will identify what type of device it is, classify it into the correct e-waste category, and provide an estimated value range based on current market rates.

If the AI isn't confident, you can manually select the category from a dropdown. Add a brief description, confirm the estimated value, and submit your listing.

photo_camera Take or upload a photo
memory AI identifies your device instantly
currency_rupee Receive an instant value estimate
2

A Vendor Reviews & Makes an Offer

Once your listing is live, verified recycling vendors on our platform can view it and make a cash offer. You'll receive the offer in your dashboard, where you can accept, reject, or wait for a better offer.

Vendors are pre-verified and their offers are final once accepted. The agreed amount is locked in — no renegotiation after acceptance.

storefront Verified vendor reviews listing
local_offer You receive a cash offer
check_circle Accept to proceed
3

Doorstep Pickup & Instant Payment

A verified collector arrives at your address to pick up the item. The handoff is secured with a unique OTP — you share it with the collector on arrival to confirm the transfer. Once verified, the cash is instantly credited to your eRecyclo wallet.

From your wallet, you can request a bank transfer or use the balance for future transactions. No holds, no hidden fees.
